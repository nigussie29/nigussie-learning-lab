# Portfolio Framework

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Institutional Standard

---

# Purpose

The Portfolio Framework defines how SkillBridge Academy documents, evaluates, and celebrates evidence of learning.

A portfolio is more than a collection of work.

It is a living record of growth, mastery, creativity, research, and professional development.

---

# Portfolio Philosophy

At SkillBridge Academy, learners do not simply earn grades.

They build evidence.

Every lesson, project, research activity, and achievement contributes to a portfolio that demonstrates mastery over time.

A portfolio tells the learner's story.

---

# Why Portfolios Matter

Portfolios help learners:

- Demonstrate mastery
- Showcase creativity
- Reflect on growth
- Prepare for careers
- Build professional confidence
- Support lifelong learning

---

# Portfolio Categories

Every learner's portfolio should include:

## Projects

Real-world solutions created during courses.

---

## Research

Research questions, reports, experiments, and findings.

---

## Code

Programs, applications, notebooks, and software developed by the learner.

---

## Data & Analytics

Dashboards, visualizations, reports, and data-driven insights.

---

## Mathematics

Models, proofs, calculations, simulations, and mathematical reasoning.

---

## Artificial Intelligence

AI projects, machine learning models, prompt engineering, and automation.

---

## Communication

Presentations, technical writing, documentation, and teaching materials.

---

## Leadership

Mentoring, collaboration, community service, and innovation initiatives.

---

# Portfolio Standards

Every portfolio artifact should include:

- Title
- Description
- Learning objectives
- Skills demonstrated
- Reflection
- Date completed

---

# Reflection

Every portfolio entry should answer:

- What problem did I solve?
- What did I learn?
- What challenged me?
- What would I improve?
- How does this contribute to my growth?

Reflection transforms experience into learning.

---

# Portfolio Review

Portfolios should be reviewed regularly by:

- Learners
- Instructors
- Luminery
- Career mentors

The goal is continuous improvement.

---

# Career Connection

The portfolio serves as evidence for:

- College admissions
- Employment
- Internships
- Research opportunities
- Professional certifications
- Entrepreneurship

A strong portfolio demonstrates capability more effectively than grades alone.

---

# Success Criteria

A learner has developed a strong portfolio when it demonstrates:

- Mastery
- Creativity
- Research
- Problem solving
- Communication
- Professional growth

---

# Guiding Question

> Does this portfolio demonstrate meaningful learning and real-world impact?

If yes, it reflects the SkillBridge Method.

---

## SkillBridge Academy Principle

A portfolio is not a scrapbook of completed assignments.

It is evidence of a learner's transformation into a creator, researcher, innovator, and leader.
