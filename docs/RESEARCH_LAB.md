# 🔬 Research Lab

## "Innovation begins with curiosity."

---

# What is the Research Lab?

The Research Lab is the innovation and discovery center of SkillBridge Academy.

Its purpose is to encourage learners to move beyond studying existing knowledge and begin creating new knowledge through research, experimentation, and real-world problem solving.

---

# Our Philosophy

Education should not end with understanding.

It should continue with discovery.

The Research Lab empowers learners to ask questions, investigate ideas, and develop solutions that improve society.

---

# Research Areas

The Research Lab supports exploration in:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Data Science
- Applied Mathematics
- Robotics
- Software Engineering
- Computer Vision
- Natural Language Processing
- Educational Technology
- Optimization
- Scientific Computing
