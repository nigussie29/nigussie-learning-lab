# What is TalentBridge AI?

TalentBridge AI is the career development platform of SkillBridge Academy.

Its mission is to connect education with meaningful employment by helping learners transform their knowledge into professional opportunities.

TalentBridge AI supports learners from their first lesson to their first job—and throughout their careers.

---

# Our Philosophy

Learning has value when it creates opportunity.

TalentBridge AI exists to bridge the gap between education and employment.

---

# Core Responsibilities

TalentBridge AI helps learners:

- Build professional portfolios
- Create ATS-friendly resumes
- Prepare for technical interviews
- Discover internships
- Explore career pathways
- Match skills with job opportunities
- Track career growth

# Platform Features

## Portfolio Builder

Every completed project becomes part of a professional portfolio.

---

## Resume Assistant

Automatically suggests skills, projects, and achievements for resumes.

---

## Interview Coach

Provides technical interview practice, behavioral questions, and feedback.

---

## Career Roadmaps

Guides learners toward careers such as:

- Data Analyst
- Data Scientist
- AI Engineer
- Software Developer
- Machine Learning Engineer
- Business Intelligence Developer
- Robotics Engineer

---

## Job Matching

Recommends career opportunities based on:

- Skills
- Projects
- Certifications
- Mastery
- Interests
