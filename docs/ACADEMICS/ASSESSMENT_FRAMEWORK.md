# Assessment Framework

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Institutional Standard

---

# Purpose

The Assessment Framework defines how SkillBridge Academy measures learning, growth, and mastery.

Assessment exists to improve learning, not simply to assign grades.

---

# Assessment Philosophy

Assessment is a continuous process.

Its purpose is to help learners understand:

- What they know.
- What they can do.
- What they need to improve.
- What they should learn next.

Assessment measures mastery rather than completion.

---

# Guiding Principle

> Assessment should develop confidence, curiosity, reflection, and continuous improvement.

---

# What We Assess

Every assessment should evaluate one or more of the following:

- Conceptual Understanding
- Practical Skills
- Problem Solving
- Critical Thinking
- Creativity
- Communication
- Collaboration
- Research Ability
- Professional Practice

---

# Types of Assessment

## Diagnostic Assessment

Determines learners' starting point.

---

## Formative Assessment

Provides continuous feedback during learning.

Examples:

- Quizzes
- Reflection
- Discussions
- Practice Exercises

---

## Project Assessment

Evaluates the learner's ability to build real solutions.

Projects should demonstrate understanding through creation.

---

## Research Assessment

Measures the learner's ability to investigate questions, analyze evidence, and communicate findings.

---

## Portfolio Assessment

Evaluates accumulated evidence of mastery across multiple lessons and courses.

---

## Mastery Assessment

Determines whether learners can independently apply their knowledge.

Mastery should be demonstrated through authentic performance.

---

# Assessment Rubrics

Every assessment should clearly define:

- Expectations
- Performance levels
- Success criteria
- Feedback guidelines

Learners should know what excellence looks like before beginning.

---

# Feedback Philosophy

Feedback should be:

- Timely
- Constructive
- Specific
- Actionable
- Encouraging

Feedback should guide improvement rather than discourage learners.

---

# Role of Artificial Intelligence

Luminery may assist by:

- Identifying strengths
- Detecting learning gaps
- Recommending practice
- Suggesting resources
- Tracking mastery progress

Final academic judgment remains the responsibility of qualified instructors.

---

# Mastery Standards

A learner demonstrates mastery when they can:

- Explain
- Practice
- Build
- Apply
- Research
- Communicate
- Reflect

Completion alone is not evidence of mastery.

---

# Success Question

Every assessment designer should ask:

> Does this assessment measure meaningful learning or only memorization?

If it measures meaningful learning, it aligns with the SkillBridge Method.

---

## SkillBridge Academy Principle

Assessment should inspire growth, encourage reflection, and recognize genuine mastery.

Our goal is not to rank learners.

Our goal is to help every learner reach their highest potential.
