# Founding Principles

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Foundational Document

---

# Purpose

This document defines the core principles that guide every decision made at SkillBridge Academy.

These principles shape our educational philosophy, software development, research, leadership, and future growth.

---

# Principle 1
## Purpose Before Technology

Technology is a tool.

Education is our mission.

Every technological decision must support meaningful learning.

---

# Principle 2
## Mastery Before Completion

Completing lessons is not enough.

Learners should demonstrate understanding, application, creativity, and growth.

We measure mastery—not completion.

---

# Principle 3
## Create Knowledge

We are not teaching students to repeat knowledge.

We are teaching them to create knowledge.

---

# Principle 4
## Problem First

Every lesson, project, course, and software feature begins with one question:

> What problem are we solving?

Understanding the problem always comes before designing the solution.

---

# Principle 5
## Learn by Building

Knowledge becomes meaningful when learners create something valuable.

Every course should include projects that solve real problems.

---

# Principle 6
## Research is for Everyone

Research is not limited to universities.

Every learner should develop curiosity, investigate questions, and contribute new ideas.

---

# Principle 7
## Mathematics Develops Thinking

Applied mathematics strengthens logical reasoning, modeling, and problem solving.

Mathematics should support learning across disciplines.

---

# Principle 8
## Artificial Intelligence Supports Human Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence should encourage curiosity, reflection, and creativity.

AI should never replace independent thinking.

---

# Principle 9
## Lifelong Learning

Learning does not end after graduation.

Every learner should continue growing throughout life.

---

# Principle 10
## Finish What We Start

Excellence requires discipline.

SkillBridge Academy values consistency, perseverance, and continuous improvement.

---

# Our Promise

Every decision made within SkillBridge Academy should strengthen our mission of developing creators, researchers, innovators, and leaders.

If a decision does not support this mission, we redesign it.

---

## Founder's Commitment

We commit ourselves to building an institution that serves learners with integrity, excellence, curiosity, and purpose.

We believe education changes lives.

We believe knowledge creates opportunity.

We believe every learner has the potential to improve the world.
