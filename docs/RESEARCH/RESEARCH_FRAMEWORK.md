# Research Framework

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Institutional Standard

---

# Purpose

The Research Framework defines how SkillBridge Academy develops learners into researchers, innovators, and lifelong learners.

Research is not reserved for graduate students or professors.

Every learner can ask meaningful questions, investigate evidence, develop new ideas, and contribute to human knowledge.

---

# Research Philosophy

Research begins with curiosity.

Every meaningful discovery starts with a question.

SkillBridge Academy believes that education should inspire learners to move beyond consuming knowledge toward discovering, creating, and sharing knowledge.

Research is a lifelong habit.

---

# Why Research Matters

Research develops:

- Curiosity
- Critical Thinking
- Creativity
- Problem Solving
- Evidence-Based Reasoning
- Communication
- Innovation
- Leadership

Research prepares learners to solve problems that do not yet have answers.

---

# The SkillBridge Research Cycle

Every research activity follows the same process.

1. Observe
2. Ask Questions
3. Explore Existing Knowledge
4. Form a Hypothesis
5. Design a Solution or Investigation
6. Collect Evidence
7. Analyze Results
8. Draw Conclusions
9. Communicate Findings
10. Reflect and Improve

Research is iterative.

Every answer creates new questions.

---

# Research Across Every Course

Every course should include research opportunities.

Examples include:

- Investigating real-world problems
- Literature reviews
- Data analysis
- Mathematical modeling
- AI experimentation
- Programming investigations
- Design projects
- Engineering challenges
- Reflection journals

Research is not a separate subject.

Research is integrated into learning.

---

# Types of Research

## Exploratory Research

Understanding unfamiliar topics.

---

## Applied Research

Using knowledge to solve practical problems.

---

## Experimental Research

Testing ideas through observation and experimentation.

---

## Data-Driven Research

Analyzing data to identify patterns, relationships, and insights.

---

## Innovation Research

Designing new solutions, products, or methods.

---

# Role of Artificial Intelligence

Luminery supports research by helping learners:

- Refine research questions
- Find learning resources
- Organize ideas
- Analyze information
- Reflect on findings

Artificial Intelligence supports research.

It does not replace independent thinking.

---

# Research Portfolio

Every learner should maintain a research portfolio containing:

- Research questions
- Notes
- Literature summaries
- Data analyses
- Experiments
- Visualizations
- Reports
- Presentations
- Reflection journals

The portfolio documents growth as a researcher.

---

# Research Ethics

Every learner is expected to:

- Be honest.
- Cite sources appropriately.
- Respect intellectual property.
- Report findings accurately.
- Acknowledge uncertainty.
- Conduct research responsibly.

Integrity is essential to scholarship.

---

# Success Criteria

A learner demonstrates research competency when they can:

- Ask meaningful questions.
- Investigate systematically.
- Evaluate evidence.
- Think critically.
- Communicate clearly.
- Reflect thoughtfully.
- Propose improvements.

Research is measured by the quality of thinking, not by the quantity of information collected.

---

# Guiding Question

Every research activity should answer:

> **Does this investigation help learners understand the world more deeply and contribute something meaningful?**

If yes, it aligns with the SkillBridge Method.

---

## SkillBridge Academy Principle

Research transforms learners from consumers of knowledge into creators of knowledge.

Curiosity begins the journey.

Research advances it.

Innovation shares it with the world.
