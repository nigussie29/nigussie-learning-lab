# 🤖 Luminery

## "Every learner deserves a mentor."

---

# What is Luminery?

Luminery is the intelligent learning mentor of SkillBridge Academy.

Unlike a traditional chatbot, Luminery is designed specifically for education.

Its purpose is not simply to answer questions, but to mentor, encourage, guide, and inspire learners throughout their educational journey.

Luminery helps learners understand concepts, develop confidence, complete projects, and achieve mastery.

---

# Our Philosophy

Teaching provides knowledge.

Mentoring builds confidence.

Luminery exists to mentor.

---

# Core Responsibilities

Luminery helps learners by:

- Explaining difficult concepts
- Recommending what to study next
- Tracking learning progress
- Encouraging consistent practice
- Supporting project development
- Connecting mathematics to technology
- Preparing learners for careers

---
