# 🤖 Luminery Architecture

## "An AI Learning Operating System"

---

# Vision

Luminery is the intelligent mentoring system of SkillBridge Academy.

Unlike a general AI assistant, Luminery is purpose-built for education.

Its mission is to mentor every learner from their first lesson to their first career opportunity.

---

# Core Mission

Luminery mentors.

It helps learners:

- Learn
- Understand
- Practice
- Build
- Master
- Apply
- Innovate

Every interaction should move the learner one step closer to mastery.

# The Six Coaches

## 🎓 Learning Coach

Responsibilities

- Explain lessons
- Recommend next lessons
- Detect learning gaps
- Generate study plans
- Track mastery

---

## 📐 Mathematics Coach

Responsibilities

- Explain formulas
- Derive equations
- Connect mathematics to AI
- Mathematical modeling
- Statistics
- Optimization
- Numerical methods

---

## 💻 Project Coach

Responsibilities

- Explain project requirements
- Break projects into milestones
- Review architecture
- Suggest improvements
- Debug strategy

---

## 🔬 Research Coach

Responsibilities

- Research ideas
- Literature review guidance
- Experimental design
- Academic writing
- Publication support

---

## 💼 Career Coach

Responsibilities

- Resume guidance
- Portfolio review
- Interview preparation
- Career planning
- Certification guidance

---

## 🌟 Motivation Coach

Responsibilities

- Celebrate achievements
- Encourage persistence
- Build confidence
- Reinforce progress
- Support lifelong learning

# Long-Term Vision

Luminery will become one of the world's leading AI mentoring systems.

It will help learners understand difficult concepts, build projects, conduct research, prepare for careers, and continue learning throughout their professional lives.

Our goal is not to replace teachers.

Our goal is to amplify great teaching through intelligent mentoring.
